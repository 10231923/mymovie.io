Introduction

Written and directed by Joel and Ethan Coen, 'The Big Lebowski' is a dark comedy. Released in 1998, the movie centres around a milk drinking, dead beat stoner, 'The Dude' who is played by Geoff Bridges. In arguably his best, if somewhat strange role. The story sees him caught up in catalogue of disasters due to mistaken identity.

Geoff Bridges is supported by a cast of fine actors, as a result the movie has rightly earned cult status.
